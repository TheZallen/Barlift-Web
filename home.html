NU BAR WEEK 2015

Win $1000 worth of AMEX gift cards #schortymapiro

Come celebrate the warm weather this week at NU Bar Week 2015! With only a couple weeks until school’s out, don’t miss the chance to catch up with old friends and get your drink on before everyone goes their separate ways for the summer.

NU Bar Week is an annual week-long bar event hosted by BarLift. We’ve partnered with several bars in Wrigleyville and Evanston to put on NU-exclusive events and to serve up awesome drink deals this week. Our mission is to bring campus together by providing a platform for people to have fun whenever they want (days like Dillo shouldn’t be a rarity at NU)

Download the new-look BarLift mobile app (iOS/Android) to stay in the loop and see where everyone’s headed for NU Bar Week.

Where is Schorty Mapiro going out tonight? To celebrate the launch of our new app, we’re giving out $1000 worth of AMEX gift cards this week! All you have to do is find out where Schorty Mapiro is going out and send him a “nudge” in the BarLift app. Be sure to follow our Facebook and Instagram for more details.

Rules: Multiple $25 gift cards raffled every day of NU Bar Week. Winners will be notified at 6PM directly through a BarLift push notification (push notification need to be turned on). Gift cards can be picked up at one of the NU Bar Week events from a BarLift rep at the bar.

DRINK SPONTANEOUSLY

FEATURES

BarLift is an app that sends you exclusive drink deals and freebies from bars & restaurants located around you.

We’re available for iOS and Android and currently live at Northwestern.

Deals are sent in real time, giving you spontaneous options throughout the night. In-app social features enable you to stay connected with the people around you, allowing you to see who’s interested in going out and where the crowds are headed.
